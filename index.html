About Focusmate

Focusmate is a platform designed to help students enhance their concentration and productivity during study sessions. Our goal is to create a distraction-free environment where users can engage in focused work alongside others in a virtual space.

Whether you're preparing for exams, working on assignments, or simply trying to stay focused, Focusmate offers tools to help you achieve your goals more effectively.
